    What to do if you think you are owed overtime

    1. Use this tool to estimate the overtime hours and pay you believe you are owed.
    2. Gather your pay stubs, work schedules, and any written agreements with your employer.
    3. Talk to your employer first. Show them your records and ask for the difference.
    4. If your employer does not resolve it, file a complaint with the Federal Labour Program.

    Important limits of this tool

    This is an informational estimate only, not legal advice. Final entitlement depends on your complete employment details, contracts, collective agreements, and official interpretation by the Labour Program. When in doubt, contact the Labour Program directly at 1-800-641-4049.
